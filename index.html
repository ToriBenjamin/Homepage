About me

I am a 3rd-year student at UC Blue Ash in my 2nd year in Media Communication after changing my major a year into my education. My passion has always been art and I have been wanting to improve my skills. I feel that college has always been the best option for me and I'd love to take my creative mind into the work field.

My art outside of school tends to focus on pencil graphite portraits that have a splash of color in them somewhere. I also indulge in drawing some original characters of mine, as well as anime characters. While I can do art digitally and I understand that it's pretty much the new norm today, I very much prefer traditional art done on paper.
